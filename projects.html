MoMa-Net: A Hybrid Lightweight Framework

MoMa-Net (Mobile Mamba Network) is a lightweight hybrid architecture for efficient multi-organ ultrasound classification. It combines the local feature extraction of MobileNetV3-Small with the linear-complexity global modeling of a Mamba-based state-space module.
